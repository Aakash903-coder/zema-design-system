# SideSheet

### SideSheets are panels that appear on the right side of the screen. Similar to Modals, they are useful when users need to complete actions without losing the context of the current page.
---
<br />


## When to use:
- To create filters with many fields on the content shown on the page.
- To create forms while keeping the main content of the page visible.
- To show detailed information about a specific item on the page.

<br />

## When not to use:
- When the content of the SideSheet is not related to the content of the page.
- Do not use SideBars as a vertical navigation solution.

<br />

## Comments:
- ⚠️ This component is designed for desktop and tablet uses only.
